Note: We have provided samples from the Official WaveGrad2 Github.io, along with our own samples. Please note that the official samples are for references only, and should not be directly compared our samples, since the training dataset is not the same. (We used LJSpeech dataset, whereas the original paper used a proprietary dataset).
